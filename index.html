Get Started

Continuous practice with the right person leads to greater achievements.

This website provides an interactive user experience that helps Japanese language learners improve their listening skills by offering audio-based practice of verb conjugations. By focusing on auditory repetition of different verb forms, it enhances users' ability to recognize and understand conjugated verbs in real-life conversations and exams, making language acquisition more effective and engaging.

What are the forms available?

This site is fully dedicated to the N5 Examination. This site will help you strengthen your listening skills with audio sessions. The N5 Examination contains 6-verb forms. Here you can master these 6-verb forms through continuous hearing practice. By listening to the 6 forms of the verb repeatedly, you can enhance your listening comprehension skills. So we request you to be patient and stay connected.
